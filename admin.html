How to Publish
Saving your changes to the live website
  1. Click Save & Download above — a file called prices-data.js will download to your computer.
  2. Go to your GitHub repository and open the folder js.
  3. Click on prices-data.js, then click the pencil (edit) icon.
  4. Select all the existing text, delete it, and paste the content of your downloaded file.
  5. Click Commit changes — your live prices page will update within a minute or two.
