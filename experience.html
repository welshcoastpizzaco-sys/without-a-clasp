What to Expect

A private, unhurried appointment from first hello to forever weld. Here's exactly how it works.

01
Enquire

Reach out via the contact form, email, or DM. Tell me a little about what you have in mind — the occasion, the style you're drawn to, or simply that you're curious. I'll get back to you personally to discuss availability and answer any questions. There's no pressure and no commitment at this stage.

02
Your Consultation

We meet one-to-one. You'll try on different chain styles, explore lengths and layering options, and talk through what feels right for you. I'll answer every question — nothing is too small. The appointment moves at your pace. We only move on when you're completely happy with your choice.

03
The Weld

Using a professional micro-welder, I'll fuse your chosen chain seamlessly around your wrist, ankle, finger, or neck. The process is quick, safe, and completely painless — most welds take under 60 seconds. The chain is held loosely in place while a tiny, precise pulse of electricity creates a permanent bond at the two ends. No clasps. No joins. Just one continuous, perfect loop.

04
Wear It Forever

That's it. You leave wearing your new piece — with no clasp, no fussing, and nothing to lose. You'll receive full aftercare guidance before you go, and I'm always available if you have questions later. Your piece is designed to stay with you through everything. And if it ever comes loose for any reason, I'll re-weld it for you at a reduced rate.

Questions Answered

Everything you might be wondering

Does it hurt?+
Not at all. The micro-welding process uses a tiny, precise pulse of electricity that you simply won't feel. The chain is held loosely around your wrist or ankle before the weld — it never touches your skin during the process.
Can I wear it in the shower, swimming, and the gym?+
Yes — that's the whole point. Permanent jewellery is designed for everyday life. You can wear it in the shower, in the sea, in the pool, and at the gym. We recommend rinsing with fresh water after salt or chlorine exposure and patting dry. Full aftercare advice is provided at your appointment.
What if I need to remove it — for medical reasons, for example?+
The chain can be cut off with small scissors or nail scissors at home. The weld is the weakest point — so if it ever catches on something, it will release there rather than cause any injury. After removal, simply bring the chain back and I'll re-weld it for you at a reduced rate.
What metals are available?+
I offer sterling silver, gold fill, and 14k solid gold chains. Each has different properties in terms of longevity, appearance, and price. I'll walk you through the differences at your consultation so you can make the right choice for your lifestyle and budget.
How long does the appointment take?+
The actual weld takes under 60 seconds. The whole appointment — including consultation, trying on styles, and finalising your choice — typically takes between 20 and 45 minutes. I never rush, so if you want longer, that's absolutely fine.
Can I bring someone with me?+
Of course. Many clients bring a friend, partner, or family member — and often they end up booking too. Group appointments are also available for celebrations, hen parties, and shared occasions. See the Prices page for group booking details.
Where do appointments take place?+
Appointments take place at [your studio/salon address] or via mobile visits for group bookings. Get in touch to discuss what works best for you.
