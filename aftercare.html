Caring for Your Permanent Jewellery

Your piece is built to last — but a little care goes a long way. Follow these simple guidelines to keep it looking its best.

The Basics

Simple care for a lifetime of beauty

Permanent jewellery is made to be lived in. You don't need a special routine — just a few easy habits that will keep your piece shining for years. Everything you need to know is on this page.

Daily Wear

Your permanent jewellery is designed for everyday life — wear it always. It is safe in the shower, swimming pool, and the sea. Rinse with fresh water after exposure to salt water or chlorine to preserve brilliance, and pat gently dry.

Cleaning

Clean gently with warm water and a soft cloth or a very soft-bristled toothbrush. Avoid harsh chemicals, abrasive cleaners, or ultrasonic jewellery cleaners — these can weaken the weld point over time. A simple rinse is usually all you need.

Lotions & Perfumes

Apply moisturisers, perfumes, and fake tan before dressing — and away from your permanent piece where possible. Build-up of product can dull the metal over time. If residue does accumulate, a gentle clean will restore the shine.

Exercise & Activity

Your jewellery is durable enough for sport, gym sessions, and active holidays. For high-impact or contact sports, you may wish to tape over the piece as a precaution — though most clients wear theirs without issue throughout any activity.

Travel & Holidays

Take it everywhere. Your piece is designed to travel with you — through airports, on beaches, through humidity and cold. Simply rinse after salt water exposure. There's nothing to lose and nothing to forget. That's the whole point.

If It Breaks

The weld is intentionally the weakest point — if your piece is ever caught on something, it will release there rather than cause injury or damage the chain. If this happens, don't worry. Simply save the chain and get in touch — I'll re-weld it for you at a reduced rate.

Quick Reference

The simple do's and don'ts

Do

✓  Wear it always
✓  Shower, swim, sleep in it
✓  Rinse after salt & chlorine
✓  Clean gently with warm water
✓  Contact me if it needs re-welding

Avoid

✕  Harsh chemical cleaners
✕  Ultrasonic jewellery cleaners
✕  Abrasive cloths or scrubbing
✕  Direct perfume on the metal
✕  Pulling or bending the chain

Remember

The weld is the weakest point by design. If it ever releases, simply save the chain and get in touch. Your piece can always be restored — and it still won't have a clasp.
